To the church
Guests camping/staying with us at the orchard, there will be friends and family cars as pick up options.

Party
At the family's orchard, near Katafyki gorge from 9 pm till we drop. We leave taverna and get to the orchard by cars.
Back to the village

Cars will be returning to the village a couple of times throughout the night.
