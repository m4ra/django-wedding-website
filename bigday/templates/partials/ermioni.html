Ermioni

Getting There and Away

Ermioni is located South of Athens, East of Peloponnese, and across the Hydra island (30min with taxi boat).

For those coming from Athens International airport (Eleftherios Venizelos), it is about 1 hour bus or metro ride to Piraeus port, from where you can take the flying dolphin and arrive in 2hrs to Ermioni. We recommend the following route: from airport take the Bus X96, stop at KARAISKAKI SQUARE and navigate through Exit 9 (see the map of piraeus port below) Take the Flying Dolphin (32euro one way, children get ticket discount). Online Booking for the dolphin in advance is recommended.

If you are interested in renting a car from Piraeus, please get in touch with us and we will help you with finding a good price rental.

For those of you driving, we will try to facilitate carpooling with other guests.
